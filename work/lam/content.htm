Look At Me 3D Scans
Agisoft Photoscan, ZBrush, 3Ds Max, Adobe Photoshop


Objective:

Make a 3D-scan and 3D-print of Look At Me editorial crew, create 3D-models of neuro-interfaces like Emotiv EPOC and Interaxon Muse and make the crew models wear these gadgets.
