Sneakers Scans
Agisoft Photoscan, ZBrush, 3Ds Max, Adobe Photoshop


Objective:

Make a 3D-scan of some cool sneakers, including:

New Balance Running 880 V4
Afour Sabotage x GF "25th anniversary"
Reebok Insta Pump Furry x Crossover
Reebok Question "Crocus"
Nike Air Yeezy II
and Nike Air Jordan 11 "Gamma Blue"

All of them were also 3D-printed as a forms for chockolate candies.
