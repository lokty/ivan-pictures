Bohemia Augmented Reality app
3Ds Max, Photoshop, Unity, Vuforia, C#





Objective:

To create interactive augmented reality app under iOS (iPad and iPhone) for premium-class apartment complex in Saint-Petersburg.

This app must work without augmented reality as well.
